Volunteer with Help Hive

Join our team of student volunteers making real change in the community — no experience needed, just a willingness to show up.

Your Role

What Volunteers Do

There's a volunteer role for every schedule, skill set, and comfort level.

📣

Drive Promotion

Spread the word at your school, club, or neighborhood about ongoing drives. Hang flyers, post on social media, and talk to people.

📦

Collection Day Support

Help manage collection tables, accept donated items, sort and inventory everything that comes in during drive events.

🚗

Logistics & Delivery

Help transport collected items to partner organizations on distribution days (driver's license or adult chaperone required).

🗂️

Event Planning

Work behind the scenes to plan, coordinate, and manage drive logistics — great for organized people who love details.

📸

Media & Content

Document drives, capture photos and videos, and help tell Help Hive's story on social media and through impact reports.

Why Volunteer

What You'll Get Out of It

📄

Community Service Hours

All volunteer hours are tracked and can be used for school community service requirements.

🏅

Reference Letters

Long-term volunteers can request recommendation letters for college applications and scholarships.

🤝

Real Leadership Experience

Take on coordination roles, manage projects, and build skills that matter beyond the classroom.

💛

Tangible Community Impact

You'll see — and feel — the direct difference you're making. That's something no classroom can replicate.

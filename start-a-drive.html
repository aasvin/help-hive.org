Start a Drive

Bring Help Hive to your school, club, or organization. We'll handle the planning — you just rally your community.

The Process

How Starting a Drive Works

We've made it as easy as possible. You don't need experience — just initiative.

📝

Submit the Form

Tell us about your school or organization, what drive you have in mind, and your timeline.

📞

We'll Connect With You

A Help Hive team member will reach out within a few days to talk through the details and answer questions.

🗂️

We Plan Together

We'll co-create a drive plan with timelines, promotional materials, collection logistics, and partner coordination.

🚀

You Launch

Promote the drive at your school, collect donations, and we'll handle the distribution side.

What You Get

Help Hive Supports You Every Step

🖨️

Promotional Materials

Printable flyers, digital graphics, and social media templates ready to go for your drive.

📋

Drive Planning Guide

A step-by-step guide to running a successful drive at your school or organization.

👥

Volunteer Support

Help Hive volunteers available to assist on collection days if you need extra hands.

🤝

Partner Coordination

We handle connecting with partner organizations and arranging item pickup and distribution.

📊

Impact Report

After the drive, you'll receive a full impact report showing exactly what your effort accomplished.
